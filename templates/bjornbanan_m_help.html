Björnbanan Commands

Björnbanan music got little different prefixes, m n and b.

        m can be exchanged for n or b for the different bots.
        mp [song name/youtube link] - plays this song or adds it to queue
        ml - leaves and stops playing
        ms - skips somethng
        mq - shows queue
        mremove [index] - removes the number in queue nr index
